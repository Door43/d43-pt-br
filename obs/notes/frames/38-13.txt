===== The Disciples Slept [38-13] =====

{{:en:obs:obs-38-13.jpg?nolink&640x360}}

**After each time of prayer**, **Jesus** came back to his **disciples**, but they were asleep. When he returned the third time, Jesus said, “Wake up! My **betrayer** is here.”

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **After each time of prayer** - That is, "Every time Jesus prayed that prayer." This refers to the three times he prayed, as mentioned in **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:38-12|[38-12]]]**.
