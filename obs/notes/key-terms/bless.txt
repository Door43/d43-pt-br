====== bless abençoar ======

==== (bless, blessed, blessing) ====

<font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>** (abençoar, abençoado, benção)  **</font>

===== Definition: =====

<font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>** Definição:  **</font>

To "bless" someone or something is to state a desire for positive and beneficial things to happen to them.

<font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>** Abençoar alguém ou algo é declarar o desejo de que coisas positivas e benéficas aconteçam a ele.  **</font>

To cause beneficial things to happen to someone or something.

<font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>** Fazer com que coisas boas aconteçam a alguém ou algo.  **</font>

  * A father would often pronounce a formal blessing on his children.
  * <font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>** Um pai pronunciaria com frequência uma benção formal sobre seus filhos.  **</font>
  * When God blesses something he causes it to be fruitful and to have a positive effect on others.
  * <font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>** Quando Deus abençoa algo, ele o faz para que isto seja frutífero e para que haja um efeito positivo em outros.  **</font>

The opposite of "bless" is "curse."

<font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>** O antônimo de "benção" é "maldição."  **</font>

[See also: **[[:en:obs:notes:key-terms:curse|curse]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:01-07|[01-07]]]** God saw that it was good and he **blessed**  them.
  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:01-15|[01-15]]]** God made Adam and Eve in his own image. He **blessed**  them and told them, "Have many children and grandchildren and fill the earth."
  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:01-16|[01-16]]]** So God rested from all he had been doing. He **blessed**  the seventh day and made it holy, because on this day he rested from his work.
  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:04-04|[04-04]]]**"I will make your name great. I will **bless**  those who **bless**  you and curse those who curse you. All families on earth will be **blessed**  because of you."
  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:04-07|[04-07]]]** Melchizedek **blessed**  Abram and said, "May God Most High who owns heaven and earth **bless**  Abram."
  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:07-03|[07-03]]]** Isaac wanted to give his **blessing**  to Esau.
  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:08-05|[08-05]]]** Even in prison, Joseph remained faithful to God, and God **blessed**  him.
