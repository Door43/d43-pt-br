====== joy alegria ======

===== Definition: =====

Definição

Joy is a feeling of deep satisfaction and delight.

Alegria é um sentimento de profunda satisfação e prazer

  * A person can experience great joy when he is serving God and loving other people.
  * Uma pessoa pode experimentar grande alegria quando ela está servindo a Deus e amando outras pessoas.
  * God can give joy even when very difficult things are happening.
  * Deus pode dar alegria mesmo quando coisas muito difíceis estão acontecendo.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:33-07|[33-07]]]**"The rocky ground is a person who hears God's word and accepts it with **joy**."
  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:34-04|[34-04]]]**"The kingdom of God is also like hidden treasure that someone hid in a field.. Another man found the treasure and then buried it again. He was so filled with **joy**, that he went and sold everything he had and used the money to buy that field."
  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:41-07|[41-07]]]** The women were full of fear and great **joy**. They ran to tell the disciples the good news.
