====== Abraham - Abraão ======

==== (Abraham, Abram) ====

(Abraão, Abrão)

===== Facts: =====

<font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>Fatos</font>    :

Abraham was the person that God chose to start the family that would grow into the people known as the Israelites.

<font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>Abraão foi a pessoa que Deus escolheu para começar a família que daria início ao povo conhecido como Israel.</font>

  * God promised Abraham that he would have many descendants and that they would become a great nation.
  * <font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>Deus prometeu a Abraão que ele teria muitos descendentes e que eles se tornariam uma grande nação.</font>
  * God made his covenant with Abraham and promised to uphold it with his descendants forever.
  * <font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>Deus fez sua aliança com Abraão e prometeu mantê-la para sempre com os seus descendentes.</font>
  * Abraham believed God and obeyed him.
  * <font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>Abraão creu em Deus e o obedeceu.</font>
  * Abraham's name was originally "Abram," but God changed it to Abraham.
  * <font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>O nome de Abraão era originalmente "Abrão", mas Deus o mudou para Abraão.</font>
  * Abram means, "exalted father," and Abraham means, "father of many."
  * <font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>Abrão significa "pai exaltado", e Abraão significa "pai de muitos".</font>

[See also: **[[:archive:ktobs:sarah|Sarah]]**, **[[:archive:ktobs:isaac|Isaac]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:04-06|[04-06]]]** When **Abram**  arrived in Canaan, God said, "Look all around you. I will give to you and your descendants all the land that you can see as an inheritance."
  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:05-04|[05-04]]]** Then God changed **Abram**'s name to **Abraham**, which means "father of many."
  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:05-05|[05-05]]]** About a year later, when **Abraham**  was 100 years old and Sarah was 90, Sarah gave birth to Abraham's son.
  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:05-06|[05-06]]]** When Isaac was a young man, God tested **Abraham's**  faith by saying, "Take Isaac, your only son, and kill him as a sacrifice to me."
  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:06-01|[06-01]]]** When **Abraham**  was very old and his son, Isaac, had grown to be a man, **Abraham**  sent one of his servants back to the land where his relatives lived to find a wife for his son, Isaac.
  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:06-04|[06-04]]]** After a long time, **Abraham**  died and all of the promises that God had made to him in the covenant were passed on to Isaac.
  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:21-02|[21-02]]]** God promised **Abraham**  that through him all people groups of the world would receive a blessing.
