====== guilt culpa ======

==== (guilt, guilty) ====

<font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>** (culpa, culpado) **</font>

===== Definition: =====

<font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>** Definição: **</font>

Guilt is the fact of having sinned or committed a crime.

<font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>** Culpa é o fato de ter pecado ou cometido um crime. **</font>

  * To be guilty means, "to have done something morally wrong," that is, "to have disobeyed God."
  * <font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>** Ser culpado significa, "ter feito algo moralmente errado," isto é, "ter desobedecido a Deus." **</font>
  * People who are guilty deserve to be punished.
  * <font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>** Pessoas que são culpadas merecem ser punidas. **</font>
  * Some languages might translate "guilt" as, "the weight of one's sins" or, "the counting of one's sins."
  * <font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>** Algumas linguagens podem traduzir "culpa" como "o peso dos pecados de alguém" ou, "a contagem dos pecados de alguém." **</font>

The opposite of "guilty" is "innocent."

<font inherit/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>** O antônimo de culpado é "inocente." **</font>

[See also: **[[archive:ktobs:innocent|innocent]]**, **[[archive:ktobs:sin|sin]]**, **[[archive:ktobs:punish|punish]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:39-02|[39-02]]]** They brought many witnesses who lied about him (Jesus). However, their statements did not agree with each other, so the Jewish leaders could not prove he was **guilty**  of anything.
  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:39-11|[39-11]]]** After speaking with Jesus, Pilate went out to the crowd and said, "I find no **guilt**  in this man." But the Jewish leaders and the crowd shouted, "Crucify him!" Pilate replied, "He is not **guilty**." But they shouted even louder. Then Pilate said a third time, "He is not **guilty**!"
  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:40-04|[40-04]]]** Jesus was crucified between two robbers. One of them mocked Jesus, but the other said, "Don't you fear God? We are **guilty**, but this man is innocent.
  * **[[:pt-br:obs:notes:frames:49-10|[49-10]]]** Because of your sin, you are **guilty**  and deserve to die.
