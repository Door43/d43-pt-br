====== Aaron - <font 14px font-weight: normal line-height: 1.4; background-color: inherit;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>Arão</font>     ======

===== Facts: =====

**<font 14px font-weight: normal; line-height: 1.4; background-color: inherit;/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>Fatos:</font> **

God chose Aaron to be the first high priest for the people of Israel.

<font 14px font-weight: normal; line-height: 1.4; background-color: inherit;/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>Deus escolheu Arão para ser o primeiro sumo sacerdote do povo de Israel.</font>

  * Aaron was Moses' older brother.
  * <font 14px font-weight: normal; line-height: 1.4; background-color: inherit;/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>Arão era o irmão mais velho de Moisés.</font>
  * Aaron helped Moses speak to Pharaoh about letting the Israelites go free.
  * <font 14px font-weight: normal; line-height: 1.4; background-color: inherit;/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>Arão ajudou Moisés a falar com Faraó para que ele deixasse os israelitas saírem em liberdade.</font>
  * While the Israelites were traveling through the desert, Aaron sinned by making an idol for the people to worship.
  * <font 14px font-weight: normal; line-height: 1.4; background-color: inherit;/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>Enquanto os israelitas estavam viajando pelo deserto, Arão pecou ao fazer um ídolo para eles adorarem.</font>
  * God also appointed Aaron's descendants to be the priests for the people of Israel.
  * <font 14px font-weight: normal; line-height: 1.4; background-color: inherit;/inherit;;#008000;;inherit>Deus também nomeou os descendentes de Arão para ser os sacerdotes do povo de Israel.</font>
