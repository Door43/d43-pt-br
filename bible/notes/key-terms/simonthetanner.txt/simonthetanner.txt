====== Simon of Joppa ======

.

===== Facts: =====

This man named Simon was a believer living in the city of Joppa. His <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>work was to tan animal skins to make leather.</font>

  * <font 14px line-height: 19.600000381469727px;/inherit;;inherit;;inherit>While Peter was ministering to people in the city of Joppa, he stayed in Simon's home.</font>
  * While Peter was staying with Simon, a Roman centurion named Cornelius came to see him. Through a vision, God had prepared Peter to welcome this Gentile man.
  * To make it clear that this man was a different Simon from the other Simons in the New Testament, he could be referred to as "Simon the tanner" in a translation, or his name could be spelled slightly differently.
