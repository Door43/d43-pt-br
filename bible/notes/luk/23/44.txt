===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It was now about the sixth hour** - "When it was about noon." This reflects the custom at the time of counting hours beginning with daybreak at 6 a.m. 
  * **darkness came over the entire land** - "the entire land became dark"
  * **until the ninth hour**  - "until 3 a.m." This reflects the custom at the time of counting hours beginning with daybreak at 6 a.m. 
  * **the curtain of the temple** - "the curtain inside the temple"
  * **was torn in the middle** - "tore (or split) to become two pieces." This could be translated as "God tore the temple curtain into two pieces."
